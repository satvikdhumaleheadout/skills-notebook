90-day CVR Trend — Current vs Last Year

Current-year CVR trended into the post window roughly flat with the pre-window baseline, then weakened gradually from mid-May onward. LY rose +0.14pp over the same calendar window — so the structural delta after stripping seasonality is −0.32pp, larger than the headline −0.18pp suggests. Pre window starts 29 Mar.

Root Cause

What broke?
S2C fell from 24.7% to 23.7% at CE level (−1.0pp absolute; 69% of total Shapley delta — the only significant funnel-step driver). The leak is concentrated on Desktop visitors browsing the dominant TGID 23604 (Eiffel Tower Guided Tour by Elevator: Reserved Summit or 2nd Floor) ↗ — Non-Geo Desktop S2C fell from 29.1% → 25.5% (−3.6pp on 4,130 post selects); Geo Desktop fell 24.9% → 16.0% (−8.9pp on 375 post selects). All four mobile cells (iOS × Geo, iOS × Non-Geo, Android × Geo, Android × Non-Geo) held flat on the same TGID ↗. LP2S and C2O on the fixed segment essentially held — this is squarely an S2C story.
Why did it break?
Last year over the same late-March-to-late-May window CVR rose 0.14pp; we fell 0.18pp despite that seasonal tailwind, so the underlying decline vs the seasonal baseline is −0.32pp — a real structural drop, not a calendar artefact. The mechanism is not upstream: every upstream signal checks out as healthy or improving. Supply is abundant on TGID 23604's three active TIDs (~946 0-2d tickets/day, ~16,652 14-30d) ↗. Price actually fell −8.8% on TGID 23604 (median $41.12 → $37.52) ↗ — a tailwind, not a headwind. No catalogue change in window. Paid Google Ads is HEALTHY per perf-audit (per perf-audit ↗) — SIS +3.3pp, CPC flat, no campaign issues. What remains is Desktop-specific friction on TGID 23604's select page: TGID 23604 has 3 active variants — 2nd floor + guided, summit + guided, summit + cruise combo — that desktop displays simultaneously while mobile collapses or sequences. Either a select-page UX/variant-rendering regression visible to desktop only, or competitive comparison-shopping behaviour unique to desktop multi-tab users (the defensive price drop is consistent with the latter). Session recordings on Desktop visitors to TGID 23604 in late May are required to localize.
When did it break?
Gradual. Daily S2C on the fixed segment held the pre-window 22–28% range through April and early May, then weakened into the 20–22% band from approximately May 14 onward ↗. No sharp deploy break on a single day. Late-May LP2S also softened on the same days (40–43% vs the 45–51% earlier post-window readings) — same-mechanism candidate, smaller magnitude.
