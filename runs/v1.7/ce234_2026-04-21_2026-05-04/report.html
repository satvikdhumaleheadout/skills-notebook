Root Cause

What broke?
Google Ads traffic to newyorktickets.com/empire-state-building collapsed by 58% — from 1,186 users in the pre week to 499 in the post week (−687 users). Paid search weekday volume fell from 206 users/day (Apr 21–25) to just 77 users/day (Apr 28–May 2), a 63% reduction. LP2S and CVR did not break for Google Ads users — their CVR held stable at 5.6–5.8%. The drop is entirely a traffic sourcing problem, not a product problem.
Why did it break?
Google Ads drives high-intent paid search traffic with ~41% LP2S and ~5.8% CVR — roughly 8× higher than organic visitors. When paid volume collapsed, the traffic mix shifted from 80% paid / 20% organic to 51% paid / 49% organic. Organic visitors have much lower purchase intent, which mechanically pushed blended LP2S from 33% to 20% and blended CVR from 4.65% to 2.59%. The product funnel (S2C flat, Google Ads CVR stable) is functioning normally. A Google Ads budget reduction or campaign pause is the proximate cause.
When did it break?
Sharp break. Google Ads volume stepped down starting Saturday April 26 — still within the pre period — and remained suppressed through the entire post week (Apr 28–May 4). This is structural, not seasonal: last year the same calendar week showed a CVR delta of only −0.18pp vs the −2.07pp observed now.
