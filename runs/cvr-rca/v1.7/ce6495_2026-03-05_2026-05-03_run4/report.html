Root Cause

What broke?
Select-to-Checkout (S2C) fell from 31.6% to 25.2% (−6.5pp), accounting for 70% of the CVR drop. The S2C decline concentrates in three experiences: UTV Raptor Tour (44% of checkout loss), Movie Sites & Ranch Tour (38%), and Jungle Expedition Tour (18%).
Why did it break?
Two concurrent forces. First, the UTV Raptor Tour was effectively sold out of near-term dates throughout April — 0-2 day tickets were zero for essentially the entire post period, and 3-7 day availability collapsed from ~260 to near-zero by late April, preventing users at the select page from booking dates they wanted. Second, for Movie Sites and Jungle Expedition (supply healthy throughout), the post-spring break shift to shoulder-season visitors brought lower-intent browsing traffic that explored but did not commit to booking.
When did it break?
Gradual erosion from Apr 4 (start of post period, post-Easter), worsening through late April (lowest point Apr 25 at 14.3% S2C). No single break date — consistent with a seasonal traffic quality shift, not a deploy or campaign event.
