Status: WARNING

What's the verdict?
YTD looks excellent on aggregate — revenue +56%, orders +106%, click-CVR +47% — but the latest trailing month shows efficiency collapsing well below tROAS targets, masked by the YTD aggregate. The +47% click-CVR lift is real on the click side, but blended ROI held flat YoY at 1.29 (vs 1.31 prior) and monthly ROI has cratered from Jan 1.61 → Feb 1.62 → Mar 1.41 → Apr 1.17 → May 0.47.
What's driving it?
  • tCPA → tROAS migration + Feb 2026 account consolidation — Smart Bidding under MAXIMIZE_CONVERSION_VALUE shifted bid logic; CTR jumped +62% (8.97% → 14.50%), click-CVR +47%, CPC up only +16%. The CVR lift is the mechanical outcome of the bidding migration, not organic improvement. ↗
  • Tour ad-group cohort drove the win — 75% of spend, ROI 1.69, CVR 5.99% (up from 4.74%). Sensible reallocation under tROAS toward highest-AOV intent. ↗
  • ROI per dollar unchanged — revenue +56% on spend +63%, so margin per dollar invested did not improve. This is volume growth, not efficiency growth. ↗
What needs urgent attention?
May 2026 ROI cliff. English campaign (tROAS target 167) trailing-8-week ROI is 0.85; Other Languages (tROAS 158) is 0.91 — 50–60% of target, far outside Smart Bidding's normal ±15% variance band. Budget-lost IS is essentially zero (constraint is auction-side, not budget-side); rank-lost IS has climbed to 79% in trailing 8 weeks. Two competing hypotheses: (a) May 2026 CM1 attribution incomplete (offline conversions still landing), or (b) real efficiency degradation under tROAS. Validate (a) first; if real, the gap implies ~$30K CM1/month leak.
