CVR Improved YoY — What's Driving It & What's Holding It Back

What drove the improvement?
CVR rose 4.93% → 6.15% YoY (+1.22pp, +24.8%) on +72.6% LP volume growth. The lift is structural and multi-causal, not single-mechanism. Within the fixed segment MB · Paid · Google Ads: LP2S held flat (+0.12pp), S2C +3.81pp, C2O +7.40pp, CVR +1.97pp ↗.
  • Subdomain microsite expansion — six new vendor/category-specific subdomains went live in 2026 (Ken's Tours, Taadidiin Tours, Canyon X, Lower, Upper, Navajo) and now carry 32% of fixed-segment LP traffic at 7–11% CVR vs the main microsite's 6.67%; Ken's Tours alone (22k users at 11.09% CVR) is the biggest single contributor ↗
  • CE-wide A2O infrastructure fix — A2O improved +5.5pp on the fixed segment and +1–6pp broadly across nearly every TGID, consistent with a platform-level payment/fraud/inventory-sync change ↗
  • C2A +5.7pp on the fixed segment — checkout-form friction fell sharply; likely a CE-wide checkout-flow deploy in early 2026 ↗
  • Catalogue maturation — TGID 40324 (Dixie's Lower Antelope) launched 2026-01-20; TGID 32732 (Secret Antelope) scaled with a 23.6% price reduction; review counts grew 3–5x on top TGIDs ↗
  • TGID-level demand reallocation — generic TGID 30270 declined while specific named tours (Lower Antelope, Upper Antelope, Antelope Canyon X) grew 50–230% ↗
What's holding it back?
The YoY CVR win does NOT translate to incremental margin per dollar — the headline is volume, not efficiency, and the latest trailing month is in free-fall.
  • Blended Google ROI flat YoY at 1.29 (vs 1.31 prior, −1.5%) despite revenue +56% and orders +106% on spend +63%. Per-dollar return unchanged (per perf-audit ↗)
  • CVR lift is mechanically Smart-Bidding-driven, not organic — tCPA → tROAS migration + Feb 2026 account consolidation shifted bid logic to MAXIMIZE_CONVERSION_VALUE, raising CTR +62% (8.97% → 14.50%) and click-CVR +47% at +16% CPC (per perf-audit ↗)
  • URGENT — May 2026 ROI cliff: monthly ROI cratered Jan 1.61 → Feb 1.62 → Mar 1.41 → Apr 1.17 → May 0.47. English campaign (tROAS target 167) trailing-8w ROI is 0.85; Other Languages (tROAS 158) is 0.91 — 50–60% of target, far outside Smart Bidding's normal ±15% variance band. Potentially $30K CM1/month being left on the table. Validate May attribution completeness first; if real, urgent diagnostic. Do NOT lower tROAS until root cause identified (per perf-audit ↗)
  • CE-level LP2S "decline" (−3.14pp) is NOT a real headwind — within the fixed segment LP2S is flat (+0.12pp). The headline number is mix dilution from the 4x organic surge (Organic LP2S 32.8% pulling the weighted average down) ↗
When did improvement begin?
Gradual ramp across 2026 YTD. TGID 40324 (Dixie's Lower Antelope Canyon) launched 2026-01-20; subdomain microsites went live in late 2025 / early 2026 (the URL data shows zero pre-period traffic for all six subdomains, so they were either launched or remarketed at the start of 2026). The tROAS migration + account consolidation completed Feb 2026 per perf-audit — that's when paid click quality stepped up. The CVR improvement is sustained throughout the 2026 YTD window with the dip arriving only in late May (the ROI cliff edge). pre_period_healthy = TRUE.
